BNW

Why are they famous?

The "BNW" nickname was popularized by the media because each of the three horses won exactly one leg of the Japanese Triple Crown in 1993—a rare occurrence where no single horse dominated the entire series.

  • The Rivalry: They consistently finished at the top of the pack against one another, creating a narrative of a "Golden Generation."

  • Anime Appearance: They are the main focus of the Umamusume: Pretty Derby Season 1 OVA, titled "BNW no Chikai" (BNW's Oath), which centers on a relay race organized to bring the three friends back together on the track.

  • IRL Connection: In real life, these three were fierce competitors. Biwa Hayahide was the most consistent, never finishing outside the top two until his final career race.
